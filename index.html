How Astro AI Works

Astro AI is a multi‑model agent that understands your intent, routes tasks to the best AI models and tools, and keeps your context intact across steps.

01

Intent Understanding

Astro parses your prompt, detects the task (research, code, writing, analysis), checks safety, and pulls relevant context from the thread and files.

02

Smart Routing

It selects the best model (GPT 5, Claude Opus 4, Gemini 2.5 Pro, DeepSeek, Perplexity) and tools (Web, Files, Personas). Switch models mid-chat without losing context.

03

Execute & Learn

Runs the calls, summarizes results with citations, and caches insights for follow-ups. Export, share, or continue iterating—your context stays with you.
